CCIS GENERAL ASSEMBLY

University of Makati College of Computing and Information Sciences has successfully conducted the General Assembly on September 08, 2023 for the student of the academic year 2023-2024. Professor Joel B. Mangaba, CCIS Dean, started the event by introducing the core values, the faculty members of the department, and the CCIS Student Council who then sworn an oath to depend the constitution of the organization as a responsibility and obligation.
